Overweight Diet Plan

Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Whole grain cereal with low-fat milk and a piece of fruit
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and almonds
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and steamed vegetables
  • Snack: Mixed nuts and a piece of cheese
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with sweet potato and green salad

Hydration

Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Aim for at least 8 glasses (64 ounces) of water.

Exercise

Include at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise, such as brisk walking, most days of the week.
